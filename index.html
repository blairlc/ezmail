How is EZ Mail better than other accessible email products?

  • Simple to use
  • No distracting content, images or graphics, simple layout and colors
  • Easy account creation process
  • Multiple options to log into account, using a password phrase or facial or fingerprint recognition on compatible devices
  • Consistent email system, no frequent changes or updates that alter the layout
  • Step-by-step process for writing an email
  • Personal mailbox and online address book
  • Automatic spam protection - only people you know can contact you
  • Clear, large fonts and high contrast for text - minimal eye strain
  • Fully screen reader compatible
  • Option to dictate email text instead of typing
  • Option to print your emails!
